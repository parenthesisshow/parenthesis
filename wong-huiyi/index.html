Wong Huiyi

Portfolio | Instagram | LinkedIn
Books are like bridges, they are the connection between our souls and minds. Through this connection, it shapes our thoughts and perceptions through the knowledge gained. However, in this digital age, many seemed to neglect print books and are treating them as an accessory just for the 'gram.

ProjectPrint aims to communicate the value of print publications/books to people and look into understanding one of the industries that have been consigned into oblivion; how they are dealing with the change of roles with print books after the introduction of digital and the dedicated efforts to publish them. 

—

Huiyi is a visual communicator who is heavily influenced by her everyday life to create thought-evoking crafts that resonate with the audience.
