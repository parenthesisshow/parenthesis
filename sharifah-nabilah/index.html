Hello, my name is Sharifah and i’m an illustrator/ graphic designer. Designing has been my passion since I was a little girl and I’d like to think that a lot of my works are playful, experimental and child-like.

I studied User Experience design in Poly and I still apply my UX skills in my works today, as I believe that design needs to function too.

I would love to pursue a career in the creative industry and take on bigger projects in the near future.
