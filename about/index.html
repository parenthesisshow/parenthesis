The Parenthesis Show

The Parenthesis Show is a graduation show featuring works from the 17th batch of graduating students from the SIM-RMIT Bachelor of Design (Communication Design) cohort.

The concept of our theme 'Parenthesis' is a vessel to showcase our individuality and uniqueness. Tiles are traditionally something very modular, yet restricting by nature. Despite that, much like us designers, these tiles can be adapted and fit into all situations. It is a symbol of how, we designers, despite being restricted by trends and capabilities, strive in situations, tough or not.
