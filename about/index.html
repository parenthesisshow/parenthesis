The Parenthesis Show

Designed, crafted, and brought to life by the 17th batch of graduating students from the SIM-RMIT Bachelor of Design (Communication Design) (Top-up) cohort, the Parenthesis Show is a graduation exhibition featuring major project works from students. The bravest dreamers, and the soulful realist - these are the designers that make up RMIT.

The show explores 'Parenthesis' as a vessel to showcase their individuality and uniqueness - it is a distillation of a year’s worth of growth and struggles. They wanted to encapsulate what it means to be limitless, and to not be restricted by trends and capabilities as designers.

Having gone through brief after brief, the show aims to portray the maturing that comes with experience from the students’ thought processes. Long nights, insane deadlines, and multiple cups of coffee later, students emerge tougher than ever.

Enter The Parenthesis Show – come meet the designers of 2019.
