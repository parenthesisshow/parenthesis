Sebelle Sharmine

Portfolio | Instagram | Behance
“I've lived and died 100 times” is a concept narrative, created, explored, and realized by Sebelle, the creator herself. In this project she shares with us a part of her forgiving heart, her curiosity for the world’s ignorance towards negative feelings such as vulnerability and pain, as well as the depressive stories of countless heartbreaks from the eyes of many she has known. It almost rings a familiar tune, the raw, unplastered feelings of self-doubt and dilemma one goes through even at the highest points of an otherwise successful, loving relationship.

—

Sebelle lives in a quaint little cottage near the Mammoth Lakes, where swans live in the summer and snow falls every time else. She is a lover above everything else, a cat lady, fashion photographer, and designer. Then again, Sebelle isn't real, you're only dreaming.
