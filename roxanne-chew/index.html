Pocket Oddity is a way for me to reintroduce tarot cards into this new age of millennial-driven self-care movement where they can hopefully serve as daily reminders for the soul and mind instead of being used a divination tool.

I do not intend to eradicate or discredit tarot cards as tools from spiritual descent, but instead to show that they too can be used as daily mementos. A catalyst for reflection and thinking.
