Felicia Per

Recollect bakes traditional Chinese pastries with innovative modern twists.

We aim to give people a new perspective on traditional Chinese culture.

We feel it is important to recollect the traditional elements of our culture and pass them down the generations.

Developing cultural appreciation would build us with a sense of belonging to our origin and tighten generation gaps; enhancing relationships within the family.

We want everyone to feel connected to home through the taste of our products.

I’m a visual and graphic designer. Enhancing visual aesthetic in reality.
