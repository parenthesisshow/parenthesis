Wong Sze Hui

Always an unusual girl with an inner indecisiveness that was as wide and as wavering as the ocean. Reading over talking, illustrating over writing.

She adores Chinese ancient histories and its culture, their detailed architectural built form, evolving art styles and stories behind each individuals. Through every stages in her growth for work, she strives to achieve better than the previous, constantly on the look-out for fresh inspirations and instead of following the trend, she rather creates unusual favorites. She is deeply in love with every piece of her own creations and strongly believes in the saying "No one else will value your work other than yourself."
