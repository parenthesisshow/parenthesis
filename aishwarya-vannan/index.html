I am an User Experience Designer. I was trained in this field of design for 3 years. However, I have become deeply passionate about graphic designing. Most of my work surrounds illustrations. I spend most of my time doing portrait illustrations of people and fictional characters. I try to convey messages through these illustrations as much as I can. My main goal is to share my perspectives of these illustrations to those who see them.
